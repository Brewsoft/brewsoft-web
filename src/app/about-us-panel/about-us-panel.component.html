Brewing Software

Brewing software starts out the same as brewing beer, coffee, tea, or anything else: a living thing.

With beverages, we start with raw materials. Whether it's coffee beans or wheat or rye, we start with a plant, refine it, and then mix it with fiery hot water to extract the flavors of the product.

With software, we start with a raw, living idea and continuously refine it. Mix in some fiery passion for development and now we're really brewing software.
